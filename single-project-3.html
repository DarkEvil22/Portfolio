A short Cinematic video featuring The Barista which is based in Antar restaurant. Antar is a Kebab restaurant located at Dandi Mall - Cairo. The place still holds the old Arabian culture as a main theme, making it one of the best places to visit if you are interested in Arab Dishes.

Play

About The project

The project showcases my ability to turn any kind of footage into a stunning and appealing content to watch. Using a popular Arabic song by Om Kulthom and combining all the steps of making drinks and desserts into one, Sound effects to make the viewer more engaged, I deliverd the old Arabic culure emotions to the viewers in a natural and not exaggerated way.
